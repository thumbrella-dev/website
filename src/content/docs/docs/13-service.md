## Become a Fan

The free tier already provides a usable foundation for online applications. Becoming a fan automatically triples your usage limits over the free plan. There are several ways to qualify:

- Star the project on GitHub, Docker Hub, or Product Hunt
- Purchase Thumbrella merch
- Contribute documentation or bug fixes

Fans get 1,500 renders per hour, 3,000 per day, and ~90k per month with 30 MB of cache and a 15-day TTL. See [Pricing](/docs/pricing) for full details.
